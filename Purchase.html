Where To Purchase A Finger Monkey?

When you are ready to purchase your Pygmy Marmoset Monkey, make sure you do some research first. You want to make sure you go to a reputable pet store or breader not a backyard breeder. It is important that you know your baby Marmy is in perfect health because they are not cheap. It is also important that the new addition to your family has been hand fed and properly socialized several different people, so your Marmy doesn't have any issues acclimating to their new surroundings or family members.

How Much Do Finger Monkey's cost?

If you look on the web, it is not hard to see that Marmoset Monkeys can cost any where from $1500 to $10,000. However, I must warn you that If you are purchasing your monkey from a reputable seller, the price will be on the higher end. If you choose to purchase a monkey from a seller on the lower end you may fall into the trap of a scammer, or you may get a monkey that is sick or un-socialized. "

Buyer's Beware!

  1. Make Sure The Seller Is Reputable
  2. Make Sure The Seller Is Knowledgeable
  3. Always Pay In Person
  4. Pay Only At The Time Of Exchange

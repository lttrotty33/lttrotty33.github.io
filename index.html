About This Site:

This site contains some basic information about Pygmy Marmoset Monkeys, better known as the Finger Monkey. These extremely cute mini monkeys have increased in popularity as pets. Although, these monkeys are cute and small, they require lots of timme, care and money! This site offers all the basic information needed for anyone who is thinking about adding this tiny bundle of joy to their family.

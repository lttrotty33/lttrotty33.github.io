Wild Marmonsets

The Pygmy Marmoset Monkey or the more popular name Finger monkey is the worlds cutest and smallest mini monekey. They are roughtly between 4 to 8 inches long and most likely weight no more than a few ounces. While these tiny, creatures are cute, cuddly and playful, they are still wild animals. These monkeys are pretty much just like the larger monkeys. They have teeth and nails just same. The marmoset monkey enjoys climbing trees and sucking the sap out, eating fruit and insects.

Pet Marmosets

Marmoset Monkeys can make great pets in the care of the right owners An owner that is experience in the care, needs and proper handleing of marmmoset monkeys will find that they can make quite enjoyable pets. However, in order to make great pet, they need to be properly trained, socialized and happy. If your monkey's needs are not met and is not properly socialized, you will find it very difficult to care for your furry friend.
